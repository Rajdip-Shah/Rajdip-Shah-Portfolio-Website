The process of building your website

01

Understanding the scope of the project

This is our first call. We talk about what outcome you expect from your website, what is success for you, any challenges you may be having. What you need me for. What do you want your website to have and hence the number of pages and features. Your budget. We know each other better and if we want to work together. Concludingly I present you a price and time according to the scope of your project and the milestones for the project. If we agree to it, we finalize the project and start working on your website.

02

Breaking down your brand

This is our first call after finalizing the project. We dig deeper into your brand, who you are, your story, your value proposition, target audience, what your audience wants from you, what value/message you are giving to your audience. By the end of this call, we have a clear understanding of what the objective of your website’s design is.

03

Content of your website

You give me all the contents for your website. The titles, copywriting, images, links, and any other information. You can also give me any design ideas or references you may have for your website’s design.

04

Wireframing your website

I present you with different variations of content layout for your website for both desktop and mobile on Figma. The navigation structure, order, scale, alignment, negative-space, animations of contents in your website, and brief on how the layout works to optimize user experience (UX). Upon your further suggestions, we will have finalized wireframe when you are satisfied with one.

05

Styling Your Website

I will place the texts, imagery, buttons, and other content on their respective layout structure. I will present you with different variations of color combinations( primary color, secondary colors, accent color ) and typography( title, subtitle, paragraph ). Once you are satisfied with the final design of your website for both desktop and mobile which will be on Figma, we have finished the design process and now we have the final design of your website.

06

Coding the website

Once we have reached a final design, I will start coding your website in HTML, CSS and Javascript. Several debugging sessions will be done to make sure your website is properly functioning, there are no bugs and it is responsive to every device.
