My Portfolio Website

~The thought process~

What I want from the website

I want this website to represent what i can do. Moreover, to reflect my personality. I like to be different, stand out and find ways to do things differently. Following this, I had to design the website such that it stands out and gets out of the general website template. Therefore, You need to feel like you are seeing something new, something different.

What you may want from this website

If you are here, it’s safe to assume that you have an awesome idea and you are looking for someone who could give you a platform, a website for your vision to come alive. Consequently, You would want to find out if I can give you the results you want and therefore want to see my works and reflect them with your expectations. Additionally, you would also want to know more about me and if I am a fun person to work with.

Challenges

The major challenge between what I want and what you want is that I am a sixteen years old teenager which may give you an idea of me not being reliable or professional. The other problem is I don't have extensive experience of previous projects with clients, making it hard for you to trust me on my capability to give you the results you want.

Solutions

Either challenge spotted above doesn’t affect my ability to give you what you want. What’s important for this website to do is build a level of trust between us. Now, how do I do that? Let the website speak for itself. This website has to be aesthetic while still being functional. Furthermore, I need to add my personal touch to it. Additionally, this website has to give you something you may need but didn’t expect to get. This could be knowing how a website works , the process with which we will work together and even the general questions you may have regarding working with a freelancer for your website as hiring a freelancer can be scary and tough to decide.
