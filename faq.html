FAQ

What is the average cost

The cost of your project is directly related to your project’s scope which cannot be determine without a formed strategy between you and me on what you want from your website. But it typpically takes more than $300 for any website.

How long does it take

This too directly depends on your project’s scope which cannot be determined before our first call. But it generally takes more than 15 days and I additionally offer you another 7 days of free edits and updates after I have delivered you the project and the website goes online.

What process can I expect

A milestones procedure is followed to deliver you your website. Those milestones are detained on this page.

What can I expect in our first call

Our first call is only to understand the scale of your website and what you want from your website. Following this, I present you with a project proposal of the price, milestones, and time. If you agree to it, we finalize your project and the work on your website begins. This is further briefed on the procedure page. You can contact me to set our first call.
